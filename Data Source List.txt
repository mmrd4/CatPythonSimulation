Conflicts for tajikistan and Kyrgistan from the linkes below
1) UCDP/PRIO Armed Conflict Dataset version 23.1 - https://ucdp.uu.se/downloads/ucdpprio/ucdp-prio-acd-231-xlsx.zip
2) PRIO Conflict Recurrence Database - https://www.prio.org/data/31?fbclid=IwAR2mhWaz2FJfbWgPflcQwx8sAU4udjTOvwbSNSZGryNL7VVfBdkzsL4ho3Q
3) UCDP Violent Political Protest Dataset version 20.1 - https://ucdp.uu.se/downloads/vpp/UCDP_VPP_Dataset_v20_1.xlsx
This data is combined into Tajikistan conflicts vs anyone, Kyrgistan conflicts vs anyone,  and Tajikistan vs Kyrgistan conflicts into 3 different columns based on the above data

The below data was pulled from the sources below and placed in separate columns
Precipitation - mm
- https://tradingeconomics.com/kyrgyzstan/precipitation
- https://tradingeconomics.com/tajikistan/precipitation
GDP billion USD
- https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/NY.GDP.MKTP.CD?locations=KG
- https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/NY.GDP.MKTP.CD?locations=TJ
Military Expenditures million USD
- https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/MS.MIL.XPND.CD?locations=KG
- https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/MS.MIL.XPND.CD?locations=TJ
Freshwater withdrawals
- https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/annual-freshwater-withdrawals?tab=chart&country=~KGZ
- https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/annual-freshwater-withdrawals?tab=chart&country=~TJK

Each data point is listed by year from 1998 to 2022.

To predict future events between 2023 and 2032 the average value for each column was taken of data between 1998 and 2022 and placed in the 2023 to 2032 data points.
